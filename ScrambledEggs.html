Gordon Ramsey's Scrambled Eggs

Description

How to make scrambeld eggs the way Gordom Ramsey makes scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 15g of butter
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Creme Fresh
  • Chives

Instructions

  1. Crack 6 cold eggs into a deep saucepan
  2. Add the butter, or use a 2 to 1 ratio eggs to butter
  3. Put the pan on high heat
  4. Stir continuously with a rubber spatula, dont whisk and scrape the bottom
  5. After 30 seconds take the pan off the heat and keep stirring
  6. After about 10 seconds, put it back on the heat, and repeat for 3 minutes
  7. In the last minute season the eggs lightly, and add Creme Fresh
  8. Plate and garnish with chopped chives
