Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Description

Recipe on how to make Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Sour Cream
  • Creme Fresh
  • Milk
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water with salt to a boil
  2. Add in unpeeled potatoes and boil for around 15 minutes or until soft
  3. After potatoes are cooked, peel and add to a different pot
  4. Add butter and milk to a saucepan and heat until its warm
  5. Add the butter and milk to the pot with potatoes and start mashing
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Add the Sour Cream and Creme Fresh
  8. Adjust more salt and pepper to taste
