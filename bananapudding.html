Banana Pudding

Description

This recipe for bananapudding is the exact same recipe that Magnolia Bakery uses for their banana pudding.

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream 3 cups
  • Vanille Wafers 1 Box
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk 14 oz
  • Bananas 5-6
  • Jello-Brand Vanilla instant pudding
  • Ice cold water 1 1/2 cups

Instructions

  1. Combine ice cold water and condensed milk and stir until combined
  2. Add the package of instant vanilla pudding
  3. Whisk together until combined and refrigerate it for at least 4 hours
  4. Whip the heavy cream until stiff and peaks form
  5. Add in the pudding mixture and mix until smooth
  6. Add layers of pudding into the tray
  7. Add layers of Nilla Cookies and sliced bananas
  8. Repeat until all layers are established
  9. Refrigerate for at least 12 hours (overnight)
